Tokyo, Japan

Explore the perfect blend of futuristic technology and traditional culture in Tokyo.

Overview

Tokyo is one of the most exciting cities in the world, where innovation meets tradition. The city is home to futuristic skyscrapers, ancient temples, and endless entertainment options.

What to Do

  • Visit the Shibuya Crossing
  • Explore the Tokyo Skytree
  • Relax in the Meiji Shrine
  • Indulge in delicious sushi and ramen

Best Time to Visit

March to May and September to November are ideal times to visit Tokyo for pleasant weather and fewer crowds.

Local Cuisine

Don't miss trying Tokyo's famous dishes:

  • Sushi and Sashimi
  • Ramen
  • Tempura
  • Monjayaki (Tokyo's version of Okonomiyaki)
